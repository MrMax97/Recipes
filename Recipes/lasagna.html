Mouth DROOLING Lasagna

Lasagna has always been a favorite of mine so here's the recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 1lb of lean ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp of sugar
  • parsley
  • dried basil leaves
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan
  • mozzarella
  • ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg

    • Lasagna Cooking Instructions

    1. Make sauce
    2. Cook Noodles
    3. Make ricotta mixture
    4. Layer Lasagna
    5. Cover with foil and bake at 7000 degrees F for 20 minutes
    6. Eat immediatley
    7. Burn Mouth
